Help Home›Getting Started

🚀 Getting Started

From creating your account to publishing your first live portfolio — this guide covers every step.

💡
Most users complete setup and go live in under 10 minutes. Follow these steps in order for the smoothest experience.

📋 Setup Checklist

0 / 6 complete

🪜 Step-by-Step Guide

1
Create your account
Visit prolioai.com and click Sign Up. You can register with your email or continue with Google. Verify your email within 24 hours to unlock all features.
💡 Tip: Use your professional email address — it appears in your portfolio contact section.
2
Complete your profile
Go to Profile in the sidebar. Fill in your name, job title, location, bio, skills and social links. The more complete your profile, the stronger your portfolio will look. Aim for 90%+ profile strength.
💡 Tip: Add your GitHub and LinkedIn URLs — they auto-link in your portfolio.
3
Build your first resume
Click Resume Builder in the sidebar. Choose a template from the 35 available. Fill in your experience, education, skills and projects. Use the live preview to see changes in real-time.
💡 Tip: Start with the Midnight template — it's the most popular for tech roles.
4
Use AI to improve it
Click the ✨ AI Enhance button next to any experience description. AI will rewrite it with stronger action verbs, better phrasing and a more professional tone. You can accept, edit or regenerate the suggestion.
💡 Tip: Free plan gives you 100 AI credits per month. Use them on your most important sections first.
5
Export or share your resume
Click Export PDF to download a print-ready version. Or click Share Link to get a direct URL to your live resume — perfect for pasting into job applications.
6
Publish your portfolio
Click My Portfolio in the sidebar. Choose a theme, customise your URL slug, and click Publish. Share your portfolio link on LinkedIn, in your email signature and on job applications.
🎉 Your portfolio is live at prolioai.com/u/yourusername

Help Home›Resume Builder

📄 Resume Builder

Everything about creating, editing, exporting and sharing your resumes.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many resume templates are available?›
Prolio AI offers 35 professionally designed templates including standard, modern, creative, photo-based and minimal styles. All templates are ATS-friendly (Applicant Tracking System compatible). New templates are added regularly.
How do I export my resume as a PDF?›
In the Resume Builder, click the Export button in the top toolbar. Select PDF — a download will start immediately. The PDF is pixel-perfect and print-ready at A4 size. For the best result, ensure all browser extensions are disabled during export. You can also export as DOCX (Word) or JSON for developers.
Can I reorder sections on my resume?›
Yes. In the Resume Builder, hover over any section header to reveal the drag handle (⠿ icon) on the left. Click and drag to reorder sections. This is useful for putting Skills before Experience when applying to technical roles.
How do I duplicate a resume?›
From the Dashboard, hover over any resume card and click the ⋯ menu → Duplicate. A copy is created instantly with all your existing content. Useful for creating tailored versions per job application without losing your original.
Why is my resume showing on two pages?›
If your content exceeds one page, the resume automatically flows to a second page — this is intentional and expected for experienced professionals. To fit one page: shorten descriptions, reduce font size (Font Size slider in the toolbar), remove less important sections, or use a more compact template like Compact Pro or Minimal.
Are Prolio AI resumes ATS-compatible?›
Yes. All 35 templates generate clean, machine-readable PDFs that Applicant Tracking Systems can parse correctly. We avoid tables, graphics and multi-column layouts in the underlying structure. The exported PDF contains selectable, copyable text — not an image.
⚠️
When sharing your resume link, note that anyone with the link can view it. If you need privacy, set your portfolio to password-protected (Pro feature) or share only the PDF.

Help Home›Portfolio

🌐 Portfolio

Build, customise, publish and share your live portfolio website.

❓ Portfolio FAQs

How do I publish my portfolio?›
Go to My Portfolio in the sidebar. Click the Publish button in the top bar. Your portfolio becomes live at prolioai.com/u/yourslug instantly. You can unpublish at any time from Portfolio Settings.
How do I change my portfolio URL?›
Go to Settings → Portfolio Settings → Username / Slug. Type your desired username — it must be lowercase, letters/numbers/hyphens only. Your old URL will stop working immediately after changing, so update any links you've shared.
Can I use my own domain name?›
Yes, custom domains are a Pro feature. After upgrading, go to Settings → Portfolio Settings → Custom Domain. Add your domain, then create a CNAME record in your DNS settings pointing to cname.prolioai.com. SSL is automatically provisioned. Propagation takes 24–48 hours.
How do I change my portfolio theme?›
Inside your Portfolio, click the 🎨 Themes tab in the right sidebar. Browse the 35+ themes and click any to preview instantly. Click Apply Theme to make it live. Changes reflect on your public URL immediately.
Will my portfolio show up on Google?›
By default, search engine indexing is enabled. Your portfolio will be crawled by Google and Bing once published. To disable indexing, go to Settings → Privacy → Indexing by search engines and toggle it off. You can also set a custom SEO title and meta description in Portfolio Settings.

Help Home›AI Features

✨ AI Features

Understand how Prolio AI's intelligence works and how to get the best results from it.

ℹ️
AI features use credits. Free plan includes 100 credits/month. Pro plan has unlimited credits. Credits reset on the 1st of each month.

🤖 AI Feature Guide

What is AI Enhancement and how do I use it?›
AI Enhancement rewrites any text block on your resume to sound more professional. Click the ✨ button next to any text field in the Resume Builder. The AI will suggest an improved version — you can Accept, Edit or Regenerate it. Each enhancement uses 1 credit.
How does AI tone selection work?›
When using AI, you can pick from three tones:

Professional — formal, polished language suited for corporate roles.
Friendly — warm, approachable language for startups or creative roles.
Bold — confident, direct language for senior or leadership positions.

You can set your default tone in Settings → AI Preferences → AI Writing Tone.
Can AI write my entire resume from scratch?›
Yes, partially. The AI Generate feature can write your professional summary, bio and experience descriptions from a job title and brief context you provide. However, for accuracy you should always fill in your actual dates, company names and specific achievements — AI generates the language around the facts you provide.
How many AI credits does each action use?›
AI Enhancement (single field) — 1 credit
AI Generate (summary/bio) — 2 credits
Full Resume AI Enhancement — 5 credits
Cover Letter generation — 4 credits
Profile bio generation — 2 credits
Is the AI output unique to me?›
Yes. The AI generates unique content each time based on your specific input. Two users with the same job title will get different output because the AI considers your full context — your industry, skills, experience level and the specific text you provided. We never reuse output across users.

Help Home›Account & Profile

👤 Account & Profile

Managing your account, profile details, password and connected accounts.

❓ Account FAQs

How do I change my password?›
Go to Profile → Password. Enter your current password, then your new password twice. Password must be at least 8 characters with at least one number. Click Update Password. If you've forgotten your current password, use Forgot Password on the login page.
How do I change my email address?›
Go to Profile → Personal Info → Edit and update your email field. A verification link will be sent to your new email address — click it to confirm the change. Until confirmed, your old email remains active for login.
How do I delete my account?›
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. You'll be asked to type DELETE to confirm. This permanently removes all your resumes, portfolio, data and account. This action cannot be undone. If you just want a break, consider Deactivate Account instead — your data is preserved.
How do I download all my data?›
Go to Settings → Privacy → Download My Data and click Export. We'll prepare a ZIP file containing all your resumes, portfolio data, profile info and account history and email it to you within 24 hours.

Help Home›Integrations

🔌 Integrations

Connect Prolio AI with GitHub, LinkedIn, Google Drive and more.

❓ Integration FAQs

How do I connect my GitHub account?›
Go to Settings → Integrations → GitHub → Connect. Authorise Prolio AI in the GitHub OAuth dialog. Once connected, your public repositories are imported as portfolio projects automatically. You can choose which repos to show/hide.
Can I import my LinkedIn profile?›
LinkedIn import is currently in beta. You can connect your LinkedIn account from Settings → Integrations → LinkedIn. Due to LinkedIn's API restrictions, we can import your headline, current role and education. Full work history import is on our roadmap for Q2 2026.
How do I sync my resume to Google Drive?›
Google Drive sync is a Pro feature. After upgrading, go to Settings → Integrations → Google Drive → Connect. Once connected, every time you export a PDF, it's automatically saved to a Prolio AI folder in your Drive.

Help Home›Privacy & Security

🔒 Privacy & Security

How we protect your data and how to secure your account.

❓ Privacy FAQs

Does Prolio AI sell my data?›
Absolutely not. We never sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use your data only to provide and improve the service. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
How do I enable two-factor authentication?›
Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. Click Setup Authenticator App and scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy. 2FA adds a second verification step at login. We highly recommend enabling it.
I see a login from an unknown location. What do I do?›
Go to Settings → Sessions immediately. Click Revoke All Others to log out every other session. Then go to Settings → Security and change your password. Enable 2FA if you haven't already. Contact us at security@prolioai.com if you believe your account was compromised.
Where is my data stored?›
All data is stored on encrypted servers hosted in India and the EU. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We are GDPR-compliant and working towards ISO 27001 certification. You can request a copy or deletion of your data at any time.

Help Home›Troubleshooting

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common issues and how to fix them. If your problem isn't here, contact our support team.

💡
Before contacting support, try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) and clear your browser cache. This fixes 60% of issues.

🐛 Common Issues

My PDF export is blank or corrupted›
Causes & Fixes:

1. Browser extension conflict — disable ad blockers / privacy extensions and try again.
2. Pop-up blocked — allow pop-ups for prolioai.com in browser settings.
3. Large file size — if your resume has many images, try removing them before exporting.
4. Browser issue — try in Chrome or Edge (Firefox has known PDF export quirks).

Still not working? Contact support with your browser version.
My portfolio isn't loading / shows 404›
Check these in order:

1. Is your portfolio published? Go to My Portfolio — the status badge should say "Live".
2. Did you recently change your slug? Old URLs stop working immediately after a slug change.
3. Custom domain — DNS changes take 24–48 hours to propagate globally. Test with the default prolioai.com URL first.
4. Cache — try opening in an incognito window.
AI Enhancement button is greyed out›
This happens when you have 0 AI credits remaining. Check your usage at Settings → AI Preferences → AI Usage. Credits reset on the 1st of each month. To get more credits immediately, upgrade to Pro for unlimited credits.
I can't log in to my account›
Try these steps:

1. Forgot password? — Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Check spam folder for the reset email.
2. Google login — if you signed up with Google, use "Continue with Google" — not email/password.
3. Account deactivated — if you deactivated your account, simply log in again to reactivate it.
4. Still stuck? Email support@prolioai.com with your registered email address.
My changes aren't saving›
1. Check your internet connection — changes require an active connection to save.
2. Look for the Save button — some sections require manual saving (look for blue "Save" button).
3. Session expired — if you've been inactive for a while, refresh the page and log in again.
4. Check if you're at a plan limit — Free plan users can't save more than 3 resumes.
⚠️
If none of these solutions work, contact our support team with a screenshot and your browser/OS details. We respond within 2 hours on weekdays.
