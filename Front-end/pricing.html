Pricing FAQs

Is there a free trial for Pro?›
Yes — all new accounts start on Free forever. You can use Prolio AI indefinitely on the Free plan. When you're ready to go further, upgrade to Pro. There's no time limit on Free, so you can evaluate the product fully before paying.
Can I cancel anytime?›
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from Settings → Plan & Billing with one click. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. You keep Pro features until the end of your billing period, then automatically drop to Free. Your data is never deleted.
What happens to my resumes if I downgrade?›
Your data is always safe. If you have more than 3 resumes when downgrading to Free, the extras are archived — not deleted. You can access and restore them the moment you upgrade again. Nothing is ever permanently lost.
Do you offer student discounts?›
Yes! Students with a valid .edu email get 50% off Pro — just ₹150/month. NGOs and open-source contributors get Pro free. Email [email protected] with proof of eligibility and we'll activate it within 24 hours.
What payment methods do you accept?›
We accept all major cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking and EMI options. Payments are processed securely via Razorpay with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details.
Is the annual plan worth it?›
Annual saves you 40% — Pro comes to ₹299/month billed annually (₹3,588/year) vs ₹179/month. That's ₹1,440 saved per year. If you're in active job search mode or use Prolio AI regularly, annual is the obvious choice. You can switch from monthly to annual anytime.
